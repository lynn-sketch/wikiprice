About WikiPrice

Uganda's trusted price intelligence platform for Kampala shoppers

The Problem

The same product sells for dramatically different prices depending on where you buy it in Kampala. A compressor costs UGX 5,000,000 downtown but UGX 2,900,000 at China Town in Lugogo. Office pants go for UGX 80,000 at a mall but UGX 30,000 in Kikuubo. Most shoppers don't know where to find the best deals because there is no trusted way to compare prices across markets, online sellers, and formal retail.

Meanwhile, TikTok has become Uganda's largest platform for product discovery. Sellers showcase products live, answer questions instantly, and close sales via WhatsApp and mobile money. WikiPrice organizes, verifies, and makes this deal information searchable.

How It Works

For Buyers

Search → Compare → Save. Find verified prices across Mukwano, Kikuubo, Owino, and TikTok sellers.

For Sellers

Get discovered → Build trust → Grow. Honest sellers with physical shops get verified and prioritized.

Verification

We physically visit shops, check prices, and re-verify regularly. Green check = someone went there.

Protection

Risk scoring, scam detection, and community reports keep shoppers safe from fraud.
